IMDB Rating app is a web application where users can find their favorite movies and TV shows on any channel or site and rate them. Also users can easily see the top 10 movies and top 10 series based on the ratings of other viewers.
To create this website we used IMDB-API from https://imdb-api.com. In this way we can pull the list of movies and TV shows from this API easily and up to date.
